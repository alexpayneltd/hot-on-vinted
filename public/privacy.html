Hot on Vinted ("we", "us", "our") operates hotonvinted.com. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These are third-party cookies used for analytics and advertising purposes. You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out via the links above.

Third-Party Content

Listings displayed on this site link to Vinted UK. We are not affiliated with Vinted UAB. When you click a listing and visit Vinted, their own privacy policy applies.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Listing data displayed on this site is refreshed automatically from publicly available information on Vinted and is not linked to any individual user.

Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK-based site, we comply with UK GDPR. As we collect no personal data directly, there is no personal data held about you that we could access, rectify, or delete. For data held by Google, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy
